# Care Nova AI: 5-Minute Hackathon Script

## Slide 1 - Opening (0:20)
Most healthcare AI demos stop at chat.

Care Nova AI turns fragmented healthcare context into one clear, safety-checked next step.

## Slide 2 - The application (0:30)
Healthcare data is usually fragmented across symptoms, vitals, medicines, labs, records, and insurance.

Care Nova turns that fragmented context into one focused, safety-checked next step.

## Slide 3 - Application modes (0:30)
This is the product experience.

Guided mode is simple and patient-friendly.
Expert mode exposes routes, evidence, records, and focused workspaces for deeper review.

## Slide 4 - The workflow (0:35)
This is why it is not a normal chatbot.

A generic chatbot answers once.
Care Nova reloads memory, classifies intent, routes to the right path, checks evidence, applies safety, and then responds.

## Slide 5 - The system design (0:35)
This is not a single AI model answering once.

The system routes each case into the right specialist path, such as RAG, Pharmacy, Scheduling, or Alert, before the reply is shown.

## Slide 6 - Retrieval and memory (0:35)
Retrieval grounds medical facts, and memory grounds patient context.

The system builds a medical query from the message, vitals, profile, and memory, then grounds the reply in local evidence and persistent patient context.

## Slide 7 - Business relevance (0:35)
This is why teams can actually adopt it.

Local-first makes deployment practical with privacy by default, reliable use on weak networks, and lower deployment friction.
That translates into faster first-level triage and more consistent patient guidance.

## Slide 8 - Safety boundary (0:35)
The system helps, but it does not overstep.

Care Nova helps with triage, medicine guidance, labs, insurance support, and doctor-ready summaries.

But it does not diagnose, prescribe, calculate doses, or perform emergency actions.

## Slide 9 - Why it stands out (0:30)
This is the difference judges should remember.

Generic AI starts cold, answers broadly, and can overstep.
Care Nova remembers patient context, routes to the right specialist, and stops unsafe medical advice before it answers.

## Closing (0:15)
Care Nova turns fragmented healthcare context into faster triage, more consistent guidance, and one clear, safety-checked next step.
